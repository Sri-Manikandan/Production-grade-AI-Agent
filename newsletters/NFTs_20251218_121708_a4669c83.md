## Key Highlights from the Latest Articles

1. **Emerging NFT Collections**  
   - **Doodles Launches Doopies**: Doodles has announced the release of a secondary character NFT collection, Doopies, expanding its vibrant universe and offering fans new ways to engage. ([Source: NFT Calendar](https://nftcalendar.io/news/))

2. **Gaming and NFTs**  
   - **Ronin Network's New Mini-Game**: The popular Ronin Network has launched a bird-inspired mini-game called "Flappy Axie," integrating NFTs into gameplay and enhancing user interactivity. ([Source: NFT Calendar](https://nftcalendar.io/news/))

3. **Market Trends**  
   - **Slight Growth in NFT Transactions**: Recent data from CryptoSlam indicates a 1.27% increase in NFT market transactions, signaling a slow but positive recovery in the NFT market amidst mixed trading trends. ([Source: Binance](https://www.binance.com/en/square/news/nft-news))

4. **Art and Technology**

([Source: Milford Daily News](https://www.milforddailynews.com/story/special/contributor-content/2025/12/16/nfts-ethereum-changing-the-game-for-artists-and-collectors/87793855007/))
