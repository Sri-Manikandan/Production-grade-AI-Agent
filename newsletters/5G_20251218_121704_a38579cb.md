### 2. **5G NTN: A New Frontier**
The introduction of Non-Terrestrial Networks (NTN) in 5G is gaining momentum. With specifications approved by 3GPP Release 17, satellite-based 5G is on the horizon, promising to extend connectivity to remote areas and enhance IoT applications. [Explore further](https://comtech.com/general/2025/12/16/5g-ntn-is-coming-but-only-if-we-build-it-right/).
