## Key Highlights

### 1. **Counter-Drone Innovations**
The U.S. Navy has successfully tested a new counter-drone prototype during NATO's Bold Machina exercise. This innovative detection system aims to enhance the military's capabilities in countering uncrewed aerial systems (c-UAS) at sea. [Read more here](https://www.navy.mil/Press-Office/News-Stories/display-news/Article/4359959/rapidly-developed-counter-drone-prototype-succeeds-at-natos-bold-machina/).

### 2. **Advanced Sensor Platforms**
Researchers have unveiled a drone-mounted sensor platform designed for mid-air water analysis. This technology is expected to support agriculture and water quality management, providing crucial data for effective resource management. [Discover more on Dronelife](https://dronelife.com/).

### 3. **Military Drone Warfare Expansion**
U.S. special operators are advocating for expanded testing sites for electronic drone warfare. This initiative seeks to increase the military's capacity to jam cellular and GPS signals, enhancing operational effectiveness during missions. [Learn more at Defense One](https://www.defenseone.com/technology/2025/12/special-operators-seek-expanded-electronic-drone-warfare-test-sites-us/410248/).

### 4. **State and Federal Collaboration on Drone Threats**
Governors have applauded recent congressional actions addressing the threats posed by unauthorized drones. This collaboration aims to enhance public safety and grant states more authority to respond effectively. [More details on NGA's website](https://www.nga.org/news/press-releases/governors-applaud-congressional-action-on-drone-threats/).

### 5. **A National Strategy for Drones and Air Taxis**
The Department of Transportation has unveiled a new strategy to promote the development and integration of drones and electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) vehicles into the national airspace, signaling a significant shift in urban mobility. [Read the full report on Spectrum News](https://spectrumlocalnews.com/mo/st-louis/transportation/2025/12/17/dot-faa-advanced-air-mobility-national-strategy).
