## Key Highlights:

### 1. **Milwaukee's Tower Breaks Records**
The Neutral Edison, a 31-story mixed-use tower in Milwaukee, is set to break records as it incorporates advanced sustainable technologies. This ambitious project aims to not only provide living and commercial spaces but also set a benchmark for future developments in green architecture. [Read more](https://www.constructiondive.com/topic/green-building/).

### 2. **LEED v5: The Future of Certification**
The U.S. Green Building Council (USGBC) is evolving its LEED certification program to LEED v5, which introduces stricter sustainability standards. This new version aims to enhance energy efficiency and environmental impact across various building types. [Learn more](https://www.usgbc.org/).

### 3. **Innovative Green Building Plans in Alexandria**
Alexandria has proposed a Green Building Plan that emphasizes net-zero energy and renewable resources. This initiative aims to reduce red tape costs and modernize the city's environmental standards, paving the way for more eco-friendly developments. [Explore the proposal](https://www.foxessellfaster.com/blog/net-zero-renewable-energy-and-fewer-red-tape-costs-inside-alexandrias-proposed-green-building-plan/).

### 4. **Recognition for Sustainable Practices**
The Green Built Alliance recently awarded the Assembly Build with the 2025 Innovation Award for its exemplary sustainable design. This recognition highlights the increasing importance of innovative practices in the green building landscape. [Find out more](https://www.greenbuilt.org/green-building-news/).

### 5. **Global Impact Through Collaboration**
The World Green Building Council continues to advocate for sustainable practices across the globe. Their initiatives focus on collaboration among stakeholders to drive significant change in the building and construction sector. [Check their latest updates](https://worldgbc.org/news-and-thought-leadership/).

The green building movement is rapidly evolving, with innovative projects and initiatives leading the charge toward a sustainable future.
