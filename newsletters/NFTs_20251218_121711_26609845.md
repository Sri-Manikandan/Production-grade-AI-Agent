## Key Highlights

### 1. **Doodles Launches New NFT Collection**
Doodles, a popular NFT project, has unveiled its secondary character NFT collection named **Doopies**. This new release aims to engage existing fans while attracting new collectors, showcasing the project's commitment to expanding its universe. [Read more here](https://nftcalendar.io/news/).

### 2. **Ronin Network's New Mini-Game**
Ronin Network has launched a bird-inspired mini-game called **Flappy Axie**, further integrating NFTs into gaming experiences. This initiative exemplifies how NFTs can enhance user engagement through interactive and entertaining platforms. [Discover the game here](https://nftcalendar.io/news/).

### 3. **Market Insights from Binance**
Recent data from CryptoSlam indicates a **1.27% increase** in NFT market transactions, suggesting a slight uptick in interest. Despite mixed trading trends, this growth is a positive sign for NFT enthusiasts and investors alike. [Explore the details](https://www.binance.com/en/square/news/nft-news).

### 4. **FIFA's NFTs on Avalanche**
FIFA has partnered with Avalanche to launch its own EVM-compatible network, planning to migrate its NFT collection from Algorand and Polygon. This strategic move highlights how major organizations are leveraging NFTs to engage with fans and bolster their digital presence. [Learn more](https://www.theblock.co/category/nfts).

### 5. **NFTs Transforming Art and Collectibles**
An article from **Milford Daily News** discusses how NFTs and Ethereum are revolutionizing the art world, allowing artists to mint their digital creations directly onto the blockchain. This paradigm shift is empowering creators and altering traditional notions of art ownership. [Read the full analysis](https://www.milforddailynews.com/story/special/contributor-content/2025/12/16/nfts-ethereum-changing-the-game-for-artists-and-collectors/87793855007/).
